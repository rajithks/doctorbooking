THERAPIST

A therapist is a person who offers any kinds of therapy. Therapists are trained professionals in the field of any types of services like psychologists, social workers, counsellors, etc. They are helpful in counselling individuals for various mental and physical issues.
Meaning:
Therapist refers to trained professionals engaged in providing services any kind of treatment or rehabilitation.[1]
Reasons:
Therapist helps in resolving below issues:[2]

  1. Issues on anxiety
  2. Issues on behaviour
  3. Issues on depression
  4. Issues on managing life changes
  5. Issues on eating disorder
  6. Issues on loneliness
  7. Issues on having complexion
  8. Grieving issues
  9. Issues on self esteem
  10. Issues due to negative thinking
  11. Issues due to chronic illness
  12. Issues on getting sleep
  13. Gender based or sexuality issues
  14. Issues on Relationship
  15. Society based issues
  16. Stress based issues
  17. Alcohol addiction based issues
  18. Issues due to suicide or self harm based thoughts
  19. Trauma related issues
Types
Following are various types of therapists.[3][4][5]
  1. Therapists for addiction
  2. Therapists for art
  3. Therapists for children
  4. Therapists for massage
  5. Therapists for marriage and children
  6. Therapists for music
  7. Therapists for occupation
  8. Therapists for physical body
  9. Therapists for psychology
  10. Therapists for yoga
Psychotherapists belong to varied fields like psychologists, social workers, psychiatric nurses, and psychiatrists
Specialisation
Therapist are basically specialised in below areas:[6][4][7]

  1. Disorders in behaviour
  2. Mental health of Community
  3. Schooling and career
  4. Related to rehabilitation
  5. Substance based abuse
  6. autism and/or autism awareness
